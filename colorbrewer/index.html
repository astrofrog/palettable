Colorbrewer

Colorbrewer palettes are taken from http://colorbrewer2.org/. They come in three sub-modules grouped by their use:

  • palettable.colorbrewer.diverging
  • palettable.colorbrewer.qualitative
  • palettable.colorbrewer.sequential

Colorbrewer maps have an additional attribute and method related to viewing the palettes online. The .colorbrewer2_url attribute is the URL at http://colorbrewer2.org/ at which to view the palette online, and the .colorbrewer2() method will open your browser pointed to that URL.
